Poutine

Ingredients

Poutine is a Quebecois dish consisting of:

  • French fries
  • Cheese curds
  • Gravy

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cook fries for 15 minutes
  3. Preheat stove to medium heat
  4. Pour the gravy in bowl on the stove
  5. Heat gravy until desired consistency
  6. In a small bowl, combine the French fries and cheese curds.
  7. Pour the gravy into the bowl.
