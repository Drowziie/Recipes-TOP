Ingredients

Poutine is a German-american dish consisting of:

  • Cornmeal
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Hot dog

Steps

  1. Combine cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl; stir in milk and egg to make a batter.
  2. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Meanwhile, pat hot dog dry and insert a skewer into each one. Roll hot dog in batter until well coated.
  3. Fry 2 or 3 corn dogs at a time in preheated oil until lightly browned, about 3 minutes. Drain on paper towels.
